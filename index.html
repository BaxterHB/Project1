Some Types of Digital Networks

In Wellington, and most other places for that matter, there are several ways to connect to the internet. The most common way people connect to the internet nowadays is through their phones or computers/laptops. in order for these devices to connect to the internet, assuming they arent on 3G/4G, they must be paired to a router that will connect them to wifi. This website takes a look at different types of phones, computers and routers.
